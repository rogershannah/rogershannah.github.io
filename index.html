ABOUT ME

I'm a fifth-year student at Northeastern University's Khoury College of Computer Sciences, pursuing a bachelor degree in Computer Science and Media Arts.

I've always enjoyed problem solving and looking at the world from a new perspective. My love for art has lead me to a major where I can combine it with my enjoyment of math and science, and I've found my creative and determined mindset has helped me to thrive in the computer science industry. The feeling when my code comes together and the ability to help others with my designs drives me to keep improving. I'm always looking for new ways to learn and am excited at new opportunities.

I have professional experience working as a software engineer at Poloniex, PowerAdvocate, and Intel's Olympics Technology Group. I am interested in the areas where technology and art intersect, especially tech art, animation, VFX, computer graphics, tools development, game development, and AR/VR. I look forward to continuing to learn and develop my skills.
